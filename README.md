# Kal-krish-portfolio
Kal Krish (real name Narala Bhanu Prakash) is a storyteller who believes imagination is a weapon and writing is an escape. Coming from a modest background, he built his creative world through struggle, silence, and observation of human emotions.  He writes dark fiction, psychological thrillers, emotional dramas, and fantasy worlds filled with heart and danger.

Long Bio

Raised without privilege or shortcuts, Bhanu Prakash found his path in storytelling through observation, curiosity, and introspection. Under the pen name Kal Krish, he creates stories that explore the unseen corners of the human mind — fear, guilt, ambition, grief — while also celebrating courage, hope, and transformation.

His writing style is cinematic, emotional, dialogue-rich, and rooted in realism.
He can switch between genres with ease:

Shadow — a psychological thriller set on a parallel world

Room No 217 — supernatural horror based on a cursed hostel

Nature-Man — a superhero fantasy with emotional core

Betting Baddhithudu — a raw, personal drama about family and addiction

Kal Krish’s stories reflect the belief that every person has a shadow, every home has a story, and every heart has a battle.
